Elements: at least one of each element, but the more the merrier a good general rule of thumb is 2-3 oz of cheese per person A Soft cheese: brie, boursin, cabaret, Robiola Bosina Due Latte A medium cheese: Gouda, A hard cheese: Cheddar, parm, asigio crackers Fruit: Grapes, thinly sliced apples, cherries, or dried fruit like cranberries or apricots. Something savory: Olives, pickles, fancy almonds, Other accessories: sliced baguette Meats: Salami roses, prosuciitto, capicola, soppressata Crudite: baby carrots, cucumber slices, bell peppers, sugar snap peas Something sweet: jam, honey, quince paste, Something spicy: hot honey, pepper jelly, spicy cheese Chocolate: Plain dark chocolate peices, chocolate covered pomegranate seeds, or blueberries, chocolate covered almonds, chocolate covered pretzels.
