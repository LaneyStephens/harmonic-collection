Entry 2 Halloween Party
Halloween is more than a holiday it's a season!

Costumes: The perfect halloween party is one where people dress up, as Cady Heron said, it's the one day a year that you can! You can motivate your guests to dress up by threatening to deny them entry if they don't (pro tip, hire a bouncer), or you can play a little nicer and bribe them with a bottle of booze or a gift card via a costume contest! Nothing like a little healthy competition to encourge people to dress their best.

Food Options:

Pumpkin soup: My Aunt Stacy always has a big pot of this on the stove for her annual halloween party and it's a hit every time! Warm, festive and easily serves a crowd! Pumpkin Bread: Another Aunt Stacy staple this bread is baked in cans making it easy to cut into bit sized slices! I've tried a lot of pumpkin bread recipes and this one has stayed on top!
