Halloween!

Costumes:

The biggest difference between Halloween and any other time of the year is of course costumes! The perfect halloween party is one where people dress up and commit to the bit! You can motivate your guests to dress up by threatening to deny them entry if they don't (pro tip, hire a bouncer), or you can play a little nicer and bribe them with a bottle of booze or a gift card via a costume contest! Nothing like a little healthy competition to encourge people to dress their best.

Food Options:

Pumpkin soup: My Aunt Stacy always has a big pot of this on the stove for her annual halloween party and it's a hit every time! Warm, festive and easily serves a crowd! Serve with crusty bread or roasted pumpkin seeds on top for an extra flair!

Pumpkin Bread: Another Aunt Stacy staple this bread is baked in cans making it easy to cut into bit sized slices! I've tried a lot of pumpkin bread recipes and this one has stayed on top!
